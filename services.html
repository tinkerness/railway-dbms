Our Services

Indian Railways offer a variety of facilities such as:

Free WiFi

RailWire WiFi by RailTel is live at 1,600 railway stations across the country. Santacruz railway station in Mumbai became the 1600th station to become a RailWire Wi-Fi zone.

Bio Toilets

Within this year, all 1,300 coaches of north-central railways (NCR) will be equipped with bio toilets. Railway Minister Piyush Goyal in March this said that all the mail express and Rajdhani trains will also have bio-toilets with air vacuum cleaning system in the next 2.5 years, just like aeroplanes..

Cloakrooms

Indian Railways provide the facility of cloakrooms where luggage can be kept safely. The facility of waiting rooms is also available which can be used free of cost by showing the journey ticket.
